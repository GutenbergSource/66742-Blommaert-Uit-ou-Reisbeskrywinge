English

This book, of which the title translates From old Travelogues: Journals and other literary sources on the Cape, is a collection of extracts from older works related to the discovery and exploration of South Africa, translated from various languages, except for those extracts in (17th century) Dutch, which have been left as in the original, because most Afrikaans speakers will be able to read those without more problems than a speaker of modern Dutch (Afrikaans and Dutch started to diverge in the 17th century and until the beginning of the 20th, Dutch was the literary language in South Africa).
